Plain Ol' White Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • A Rice Cooker

Directions

  1. Using your rice cooker's directions measure out the desired number of servings of rice

  2. Wash your rice in a strainer under flowing water to remove excess starch

  3. Using your rice cooker's directions add rice and water to the rice cooker bowl

  4. Start your rice cooker and wait

  5. Seriously, that's it. Rice isn't complicated.
